WIP T Barriers

A sneak peek of an upcoming set of products:


Mid-height T-barriers! They are sturdy and very nice for blocking line of sight across a battlefield.


Really high T-barriers! Blocks LoS from infantry transports mounted with heavy weapons, like Razorbacks. Check out the angled sides, it allows you to build angled walls while still making sure all line of sight is blocked.  There are shorter versions of the same type being developed.
